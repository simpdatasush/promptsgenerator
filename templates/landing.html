Sample Prompts

Raw Prompt Idea:

"write a short story about a detective solving a mystery in space"

Polished Prompt:

"Draft a concise science fiction short story (approx. 300 words) featuring a space detective investigating a mysterious disappearance aboard a derelict starship. Include elements of suspense and a surprising twist ending."

Raw Prompt Idea:

"how to make a really good cup of coffee at home"

Polished Prompt:

"Provide a detailed, step-by-step guide on brewing an exceptional cup of coffee at home using common methods (e.g., French press, pour-over). Include tips on bean selection, grind size, water temperature, and brewing ratios."

Raw Prompt Idea:

"code a simple calculator in python"

Polished Prompt:

"Write a Python script for a command-line calculator that supports addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Implement robust error handling for invalid input and division by zero. Structure the code with functions for each operation."
